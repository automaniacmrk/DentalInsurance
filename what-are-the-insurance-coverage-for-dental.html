What are the insurance coverage for dental?

Different dental insurance plans cover different services. The most basic plan covers common preventative and treatment services, with or without co-pays.

  • Check-ups
  • Cleaning your teeth
  • Cavity fillings (using silver/amalgam)
  • X-rays

Other plans may provide more extensive coverage, helping to pay for major dental procedures, including:

  • Root canals
  • Extractions
  • Crowns
  • Bridges
  • Braces
  • Dental implants

If there are certain services or procedures you'd like to see included in your dental insurance, check the details of each plan during the quoting process.
