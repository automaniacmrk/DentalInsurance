Eligibility

Eligible: All employees who are eligible to receive full benefits as well as retirees

Who's covered: You and your eligible family members

Who pays the premium: UC and retirees subject to graduated eligibility

Dependent Eligibility

You may enroll your legal spouse, domestic partner, and dependent children up to age 26, when you experience a family status change, or during the annual Open Enrollment period.

About our Dental Insurance Plans

Chamber of Commerce offers four tiers of dental plans to cover a variety of needs and budgets: Diamond, Achiever, Core, and Starter**.

Union-Sponsored Dental Plans: Bargaining Units 5 And 6

Every eligible Bargaining Unit 6 employee must enroll in Trust plans, except if they are covered by other state-sponsored dental insurance through their spouse.

Bargaining Unit 5 or 6 employees should contact the Benefit Trust to find out more about their benefits and premiums for their union-sponsored dental plans.
